About Me

My name is Liam Loging, and I am an online exercise trainer who creates individualized exercise programs for my clients. I have received my Bachelor's of Science in Exercise and Sports Science and am currently in the last semester of my Master's of Science in Sports Science. I am also a certified sports nutritionist under the International Society of Sports Nutrition (C-ISSN).

My passion is to improve people's lives through fitness! I have experience with both novice and professional athletes of several sports. My commitment is to empower you to reach your fitness and health goals through individualized coaching, nutrition recommendations, and the forming of positive/healthy daily habits.
